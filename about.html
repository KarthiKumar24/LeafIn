ABOUT US

Welcome to Plantie, where passion for plants meets a vibrant community of nature enthusiasts. At Plantie,we’re a thriving hub for anyone looking to add a touch of green to their life. Our journey began with a simple idea: to make plant care accessible and enjoyable for everyone.

Our Mission

We aim to inspire and educate plant lovers by providing the best resources, expert advice, and top-quality plants. We believe that nurturing plants is not only a rewarding hobby but also a way to enhance your well-being and connect with nature.

Our Story

Monisha and Reyas, a couple passionate about nature, started a business called LeafIn in the small town of Madurai. Inspired by the beauty of fall foliage. Beginning with sales at local markets, their high-quality plants and custom arrangements gained popularity.

They expanded their offerings to include online sales and leaf preservation services for sentimental keepsakes. Leafy Treasures became a beloved brand, inspiring others to appreciate nature's simple wonders. Reyas nodded, excitement growing in his eyes. "And we could offer services like leaf preservation and custom leaf arrangements for events and home decor!"
